### 1. The Core Architecture: 5-Bucket Sequential Drain
The engine operates on a 15-minute slot simulation (6:00 AM to Midnight). Energy is drawn from reservoirs in a strict hierarchy:

1.  **Gut (Exogenous)**: Current food being absorbed. High gut activity spikes insulin and blocks fat access.
2.  **Fat Faucet (Adipose)**: Rate-limited by the **Alpert Number** (sustainable fat kcal per day). Access is multiplied by **Efficiency** (Insulin/HRV/Caffeine).
3.  **Liver Glycogen**: The metabolic "buffer." Capped at 400 kcal. Drains when gut/fat cannot meet BMR.
4.  **Muscle Glycogen**: The high-intensity reserve. Primary fuel for anaerobic exercise. 
5.  **Muscle Protein (Catabolism)**: True last resort. Burning muscle tissue triggers severe score penalties.
